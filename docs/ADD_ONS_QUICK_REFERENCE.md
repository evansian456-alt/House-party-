# Add-ons Quick Reference Guide

Quick answers to "What do add-ons do when I buy them?"

## 🎨 Visual Packs (£2.99-£3.99)

**What they are**: DJ visual effects for your party

**When you buy**:
- ✅ Added to your collection forever
- ✅ Activate from your Visual Packs store
- ✅ All phones in party see the effects

**How they work**:
- Only one can be active at a time
- Switch between owned packs anytime
- DJ controls activation

**Available**:
- Neon (£3.99) - Electric neon visuals
- Club (£2.99) - Dark club vibes
- Pulse (£3.49) - Rhythmic pulse effects

## ✨ Profile Upgrades (£1.99-£2.99)

**What they are**: Cosmetic enhancements to your profile

**When you buy**:
- ✅ Automatically applied to your profile
- ✅ Active in ALL parties you join
- ✅ Yours forever, always visible

**How they work**:
- All upgrades stack together (can have all 4 active)
- No activation needed - always on
- Visible whether you're DJ or guest

**Available**:
- Verified Badge (£1.99) - Blue checkmark
- Crown Effect (£2.99) - Crown icon
- Animated Name (£2.49) - Glowing name
- Reaction Trail (£1.99) - Trail effect

## ⚡ Party Extensions (£0.99-£1.49)

**What they are**: Temporary party boosters

**When you buy**:
- ✅ Applied instantly to current party
- ✅ Effect lasts only this session
- ✅ Can buy multiple (they stack)

**How they work**:
- Buy during party when needed
- Effect ends when party ends
- Each new party needs new extensions

**Available**:
- Add 30 Minutes (£0.99) - Extend party time
- Add 5 Phones (£1.49) - Increase capacity

**Example**: Party Pass gives 2 hours, 4 phones. Buy "Add 30 Minutes" + "Add 5 Phones" = 2.5 hours, 9 phones for this party!

## 🔥 Hype Effects (£0.49-£1.49)

**Available**:

## ❓ Common Questions

**Do I need add-ons?**
No! They're optional enhancements.

**What's permanent vs temporary?**
- Permanent: Visual Packs, DJ Titles, Profile Upgrades
- Temporary: Party Extensions (session), Hype Effects (single use)

**Can I use add-ons in any party?**
- Visual Packs, DJ Titles, Profile Upgrades: YES (all parties)
- Party Extensions: Only current session
- Hype Effects: Any party (until consumed)

**Can guests trigger add-ons?**
- Visual Packs: DJ only
- DJ Titles: Always visible (no trigger)
- Profile Upgrades: Always visible (no trigger)
- Party Extensions: DJ only
- Hype Effects: DJ only

**What's the best value?**
- Best overall: Profile Upgrades (all 4 for £9.46 = permanent, stack, always visible)
- Best bang-for-buck: Confetti Blast (£0.49 for an epic party moment)
- Best investment: Visual Packs (£10.47 for all 3 = professional party vibes)
